Oksana
Contemporary, individual and versatile cut, colour and styling in St Heliers, central Auckland. Founded in 1999 by award-winning hairdresser Oksana Patrela, who leads the salon team, Oksana is home to some of New Zealand’s most exciting young and established stylists. Pairing precision craft with intuitive and naturalistic feeling, Oksana's stylists create beautifully personalised styles informed by the salon’s long history of work with New Zealand’s fashion industry.

About

Lida Yudina is a writer and a distinguished painter especially known for her paintings of domestic and suburban landscapeIn 1967 she graduated from The National Academy of Fine Art and Architecture of Ukraine (Master of Fine Arts in Oil Painting), where she was influenced by the works of the prominent impressionists.

Lidiya Yudina is focused on still-lives and landscapes, painted on location (an plein air).
